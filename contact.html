Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting GWIC

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to emails within 24 hours during working days. For urgent matters, please clearly mark your email as "URGENT" in the subject line.

Is my communication confidential?

Yes, all communications with GWIC are treated with strict confidentiality. We only share information with your explicit consent or in cases where safety is a concern.

Can I remain anonymous when contacting you?

While we encourage you to provide contact information for follow-up, you can submit feedback or general inquiries anonymously if you prefer.

What if I need immediate help?

For immediate mental health support, email us at gwic@gnlu.ac.in with "URGENT" in the subject line. For crisis situations, also contact emergency services.
