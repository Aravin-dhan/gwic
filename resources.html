Self-Help Resources

Tools and techniques you can use on your own wellness journey

Mindfulness & Meditation

Practice mindfulness techniques to reduce stress and improve focus.

  • Daily 10-minute meditation
  • Breathing exercises
  • Body scan techniques

Physical Wellness

Maintain physical health to support mental well-being.

  • Regular exercise routine
  • Adequate sleep schedule
  • Balanced nutrition

Academic Balance

Strategies for managing academic stress effectively.

  • Time management techniques
  • Study-break balance
  • Goal setting methods

Social Connections

Build and maintain supportive relationships.

  • Join study groups
  • Participate in activities
  • Maintain family connections
